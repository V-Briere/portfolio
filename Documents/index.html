About Me

Hello! I'm Victor, a passionate web developer with expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I'm passionate about building things that make a difference, whether it's my own side projects or collaborating with fantastic companies. My recent experiences include contributing to the future of payments at Atom and diving deep into web engineering at Railtown, where I had the exciting opportunity to help rethink modern banking for millions of users (a journey that started in product design!).

I also had the privilege of being the first UI Engineering hire at Toto, where I focused on laying the foundation for their design system and ensuring a high bar for the user experience. My sweet spot has always been where thoughtful design meets robust engineering, all in service of creating products people love.

If you're interested in connecting, please feel free to reach out.
